Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

01. OVERVIEW

Vertex Sentinel ("the App") is a health monitoring application developed by Vertex Coders LLC. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and health data when you use our Wear OS and Android applications.

02. DATA WE COLLECT

The App collects the following data from your wearable device sensors:

  • Heart Rate (BPM) — real-time via wearable sensors
  • Blood Oxygen Saturation (SpO2) — via wearable sensors
  • Stress Level — estimated via heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Accelerometer data — for fall detection

03. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Your health data is used exclusively to:

  • Display real-time vital signs on your device
  • Detect anomalies and generate health alerts
  • Send anonymized data to our AI backend for medical protocol generation
  • Generate immediate health recommendations via Gemma 4 AI

We do NOT: sell your data to third parties, store your personal identity alongside health data, or share your data with insurance companies or employers.

04. DATA TRANSMISSION

Health data is transmitted to our backend hosted on Hugging Face Spaces (oracle-hub-backend.hf.space) via encrypted HTTPS connection for AI analysis. Data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers.

05. DATA STORAGE

  • Health data is stored locally on your device only
  • AI analysis results are temporarily cached in app memory
  • No personal health records are stored on external servers

08. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Vertex Sentinel is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

09. MEDICAL DISCLAIMER

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Vertex Sentinel is NOT a medical device. The AI-generated health protocols are for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.

10. YOUR RIGHTS

  • Access your health data stored on your device
  • Delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of AI analysis by using the app in offline mode
  • Request information about how your data is processed

11. SECURITY

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • No permanent storage of health data on external servers
  • No user authentication data collected
  • Real-time processing with no data retention

12. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes via app update notes on the Google Play Store. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
