# Privacy Policy — Vertex Sentinel
**Last updated:** April 17, 2026
**Developer:** Vertex Coders LLC — Denis Ijcu

---

## 1. Overview
Vertex Sentinel ("the App") is a health monitoring application developed by 
Vertex Coders LLC. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect 
your personal and health data.

---

## 2. Data We Collect
The App collects the following data from your wearable device sensors:

- **Heart Rate (BPM)** — via wearable sensors
- **Blood Oxygen Saturation (SpO2)** — via wearable sensors
- **Stress Level** — estimated via heart rate variability
- **Accelerometer data** — for fall detection

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data
Your health data is used exclusively to:

- Display real-time vital signs on your device
- Detect anomalies and generate health alerts
- Send anonymized data to our AI backend (Google Gemma 4 via 
  Hugging Face) for medical protocol generation
- Generate immediate health recommendations

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your data to third parties
- Store your personal identity alongside health data
- Share your data with insurance companies or employers

---

## 4. Data Transmission
Health data is transmitted to our backend hosted on Hugging Face Spaces 
(`Denisijcu-oracle-hub-backend.hf.space`) via encrypted HTTPS connection 
for AI analysis. Data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored 
on our servers.

---

## 5. Data Storage
- Health data is stored **locally on your device only**
- AI analysis results are temporarily cached in app memory
- No personal health records are stored on external servers

---

## 6. Third-Party Services
The App uses the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Google Health Services | Sensor data access | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| Hugging Face | AI model hosting | [HuggingFace Privacy](https://huggingface.co/privacy) |
| Google Gemma 4 | Health analysis AI | [Google AI Policy](https://ai.google/responsibility/privacy/) |

---

## 7. Permissions Required
The App requires the following permissions:

- `BODY_SENSORS` — to read heart rate and SpO2 from wearable
- `BODY_SENSORS_BACKGROUND` — for continuous monitoring
- `INTERNET` — to communicate with AI backend
- `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` — to restart monitoring after reboot

---

## 8. Children's Privacy
Vertex Sentinel is not intended for users under 13 years of age. 
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

---

## 9. Medical Disclaimer
⚠️ **IMPORTANT:** Vertex Sentinel is NOT a medical device. The AI-generated 
health protocols are for informational purposes only and should not replace 
professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a 
qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.

---

## 10. Your Rights
You have the right to:

- Access your health data stored on your device
- Delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app
- Opt out of AI analysis by using the app in offline mode

---

## 11. Security
We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- No permanent storage of health data on external servers
- No user authentication data collected

---

## 12. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users 
of significant changes via app update notes on the Play Store.
